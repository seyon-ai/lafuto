✦ What is LeUHaute™?

LeUHaute is LaTAFU's exclusive AI teacher intelligence system. Our AI conducts a real interview with every teacher and scores them across 5 dimensions: Teaching Clarity, Subject Mastery, Student Patience, Problem Solving, and Communication. Pro students can see these scores and make smarter hiring decisions.
